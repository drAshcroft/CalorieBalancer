Salmon Loaf

6 Servings
  • 1/4 cup, chopped Onions, raw
  • 1 tsp Dill weed, dried, Spices
  • 1 tablespoon Oil, olive, salad or cooking
  • 1 large Whole Egg raw fresh
  • 1 cup Bread crumbs, dry, grated, plain
  • 13 oz Salmon, pink, canned
  • 6 slice (1 oz) Swiss Cheese
  • 1/4 cup Soy milk, fluid


Instructions

Saute onions, dillweed and oil. Set aside. Mixe Egg, bread crums, milk, and salmon in a bowl. Add the Sauted mixture. Mix well. Shape into a 6X3 inch loaf in a Greased shallow baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes. Top with Cheese slices. Makes 6 servings.
