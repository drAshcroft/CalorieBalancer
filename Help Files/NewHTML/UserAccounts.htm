User Accounts

      User accounts are easy to manage.  Just a few mouse clicks to make a new account, delete an account or to make necessary edits.

How to I log in?


   If you do not have an account you must make a new account.  Click on the Make New User button and follow the instructions there.  Then enter your username and password.

If you already have a username and password, enter those.

If you have forgotten your password, it is best to set up a new account.

How do I make a new user?


    If you are at the login page,  click Make New User then follow the instructions there.

   If you are already logged in as a different user, click File then Make New User and follow the wizards instructions.

How do I  edit my account?

    To edit your account, log in as normal.  Then click File and Edit Current User.  You can change the account from this page, or return to the wizard by clicking the Edit with Wizard link.

How do I change my daily calories?

     Log in as normal.  Click File and then Edit Current User.  Scroll down the page and adjust Daily Calorie Rations to your liking.

How do I delete an account?

     Log in to the account.  Click File and then Delete Current User.  The program will ask if you are sure and then will delete every trace of the deleted user from the database.  You will then be sent to the login page.

How do I change the nutrients that I track every day?


    You make wish to track different nutrients to help you meet your personal goals.  You can adjust the nutrients that are tracked by clicking File, and then Edit Current User.  Scroll down the page and find the section that shows all the nutrients.  Click the nutrients that you wish to track and then click save at the bottom of the page.

How do change to a different user?

     Click File and then Change to Different User.  The program will log out and then allow you to start a new user.

What if I want my meals to be available to all users.

     
    The family meals section shows for all users of the program.  To move a meal from personal use to family user.  Click and drag the meal from the personal area to the desired meal in the family section.
