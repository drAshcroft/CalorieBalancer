You have successfully installed the program and now are confronted with a login in screen.  We will help you get your profile set up you just have to chose the correct situation.

I just want to sign up on my computer.

To make a new login, click the Make New Profile  The program will ask if you wish to sign up on the web.  We recommend that you do this as it makes matters less complicated if you do wish to sign up later.  If you still do not wish to be a member of the website, click No.  You will then be directed to the programs profile page.  Fill in all the information and your profile will be built.
