How do I get to the meal planner?


On the tool bar at the left is a button that resembles a calendar.  This is the meal planner.  Just click once and wait a moment as the planner is loading. The program is large so it takes a moment for all the componites to load. 

How do I place the meals?  

     After a meal has been created as shown here, It can be placed in the calorie counter.  This is done by using the toolbar on the right.  After selecting the desired meal, you can either drag the meal to the calorie counter, or double click the meal.  The meal will then be automatically loaded according the options set in the Options menu at the top of the page.

How do I make new meals?

     There are three ways to enter a meal.  The first is to enter it using the calorie counter.  Enter the meal name, i.e. Breakfast, Brunch, Lunch, Snack, Dinner, Treat.  Then enter the foods.  Last click Make Into Meal.  You will be taken to the new meal dialog.  You need to enter a meal name and then can enter any of the other information required such as information or description.  Click the Ingredients tab to make sure that all the ingredients have been entered and then click save at the top of the page.

     The second way to enter a meal is to right click on the meal toolbar to the right.  A popup menu will appear.  Click New Meal.  The new meal dialog will appear and then fill in all the information.  Make sure to input ingredients.  Then click save.  The meal will be inserted into the meal toolbar on the right.

     The third way to enter a meal is to click on the new meal button on the right and then follow the above instructions for entering a meal.

 

How do I edit existing meals?


    Select the meal you wish to edit, right click the meal and select Edit Meal.  Then you can change anything in the meal that needs to be changed and then press Save at the top of the page when you are done.

How do I delete a meal?


         Select the meal that you desire to delete.  Right click the meal, and then select Delete Meal.  The meal will then be deleted.

How do I get a quick summary of the meal?

        Select the meal that you wish to view, right click the meal and select View Meal.  A short summary will show on the screen.

How do I move a meal?

       Moving a meal from one cell to another is quite easy.  Just press the Shift key and drag the meal to a new cell.

How do I remove a meal from the planner?

       To remove a meal from the planner, click the meal and the press delete.  To delete multiple meals, hilight the desired area, and then press delete.

How do I copy/paste meals?

      To copy meals, just hilight the meals and then press CTRL-C, or CTRL-X to copy or cut the meals.  To paste select the upper left hand corner and then press CRTL-V.

How do I download a plan?

      The Website has mealplans that you can download.  Press the button to navigate to the webpage.   Click the Diet & Fitness plans link and then you can select the plan that you would like.  Click the Download link.  These are large files and can take a while to download.  You will be asked for a start date.  This is the day that you would like to begin this new plan.  The program will then put all the information into the calorie counter so you can begin to live your new diet.

How do I print a plan?

     To print a plan, press the calendar button, ,  so the plan is on the screen. Then File and Print.  You can also get print off the instructions to the recipes that are used today and the instructions for the meals.

How do I print a shopping list for the plan?

     To print a shopping list based on the meals that have been selected, click File and then Shopping List.  You will be taken to a new dialog.  The dialog will, by default, list the shopping needs for this week.  You can select a different period of time by clicking a new start date (to the left) and new end date (to the right) and then clicking Print or Print Preview.
