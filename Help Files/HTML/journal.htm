Journal

         The journal is designed to help you track all aspects of your diet.  You can track your hunger, weight loss and emotions.  Click on the journal button to the left and then a new window will show up.  Enter in all the information that you can for the day. 

The body fat percentage will be calculated from your measurements, or if you have a scale you can enter it directly.  The results for the day will be shown to the right of the page.

Next you should enter the feedback of your diet.  1=little and 10=lot.  This information will help you to make educated guesses about the amount of food you should eat to lose weight.  

The journal at the bottom of the page is recommended to help you deal with your emotions in a constructive manner.  Use the journal to express the feeling you have and to de stress at the end of the day.
