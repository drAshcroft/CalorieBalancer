A quick start

We will quickly guide you through the most important features of this program. 

  First you need to make sure that your profile is correct.

Click on Tools at the top of the program and then on Profile.  You will be taken to a new window that contains all your information.  Look through the information to make sure that it is correct.  You can change anything that is incorrect as needed.  Click Done to exit.

 

Load a mealplan.

            Click the meal plan button on the left.  After a moment, the window will pop up to show you a weekly calendar. 

 

On the left are two boxes, the top for the plans and the bottom for the meals.  Double click on breakfast to see all the meals it contains.  Click on one of the meals and the bottom of the page will show a short description.
  Right click on the meal and a popuop menu will appear to show you the meal options. 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Now that you have seen these options, click and drag the meal to the top box (breakfast) on today’s date.  The bottom of the day will be updated with the total calories for the day and the calories balance.  You can use this to help you plan the weeks meals.

Now you are done.  Click File/Save  at the top of the page and then File/Exit.  You will see the meal in the main calorie tracker window.

 

Enter your calories

            Now you are ready to enter foods.  Click on an empty space under foodname.  Enter the word lunch.  Select the “Lunch” item, the line will be grayed out and then add “hamburger”.

Choose the Burger King item, next you will be prompted for a unit.  Chose “item”, or you can weight your sandwich.  Now just enter “1” serving.  You can repeat this process as necessary.  The program will show the calories for this item to the right.

The bottom of the display shows the calories, nutrients and balance of the day.  This display is continuously updated.  Once the calorie bar hits the second green line, you have met your daily calories.

Enter Your Exercise

            Click on the exercise button to the left.  You will be shown a week grid.  On the left are the exercise names. 

           

Click the space under exercise.  Type in “Walking”.  You will see many choices, but chose “Walking(steps)”.  ‘steps’ refers to how any steps you have taken in that day(for those who like pedometers).  If you do not count your steps, chose “Walking (Speed/Minutes).  This allows you to put your speed and time in to the program.  If there are no parenthesis, you should just enter in the minutes.  This is shown in the picture.

Next you must enter your numbers.  Click on the appropriate day and space.  In the first example, the number of steps has been entered.  In the second, the speed and minutes have been entered.  And last, the number of minutes were added.

At the bottom of the page we can see that number of calories burned in these activities is shown.  This number is subtracted from the calories shown in the bars.  This represents the fact that it is only the total number of calories consumed and burned in a day that matters.

Record your day

            You are now ready to make a journal entry.  Click the entry button and a new window will pop up.

Now enter your weight, and measurements.  You do not have to enter the body fat percentage unless you have a scale that can measure this.  The program will calculate the value from your weight and measurements.

You will be shown your daily information in calories and pounds per week change. 

Now you enter your discomfort with the diet.  10 means lots. 1 means none.  You can also write in the journal as needed.  We may build a blogging service to help you get the support you need, however, this service is not built yet.

Conclusion

            You now know how to use all the basic features of this program.  You are set to complete the only diet that always works.
