Login
You have successfully installed the program and now are confronted with a login in screen.  We will help you get your profile set up you just have to chose the correct situation.

I Signed up on the Web already.

If you have signed up on the web, you already have a username and password.  Enter these into the login form and then click Ok.  The program will indicate that it cannot find your profile on this local computer and will ask if you want to look for your profile on the web.  Click Yes and your profile will be loaded and you will be allowed to use the program. 

I want to sign up for the website and on my computer.

To sign into the program and the website, click Make New Profile.  The program will ask if you wish to sign up on the web.  Click Yes and then follow the instructions on the website.  When you are done with the web signup, you will have an appropriate username and password.  Put these into the program.  The program will ask if you wish to look for your profile on the internet.  Click Yes and your profile will be downloaded and you will have access to all the features of the program.

I just want to sign up on my computer.

To make a new login, click the Make New Profile  The program will ask if you wish to sign up on the web.  We recommend that you do this as it makes matters less complicated if you do wish to sign up later.  If you still do not wish to be a member of the website, click No.  You will then be directed to the programs profile page.  Fill in all the information and your profile will be built.
