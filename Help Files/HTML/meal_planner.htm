The Meal Planner

          The meal planner helps you to take control of your eating.  Need a little help eating healthy?  Just download the healthy eating plan.  Do you get home and end up eating what is easy because you can figure out what you want?  The meal planner is the solution.

How do I get to the meal planner?

On the tool bar at the left is a button that resembles a calendar.  This is the meal planner.  Just click once and wait a moment as the planner is loading. The program is large so it takes a moment for all the componites to load. 

How do I place the meals?

Double Click the desired time of day to show all the meals.  Then click and drag the meal to the desired day and meal time.  The meal will drop right into the space.  You can move the meals around in the calendar by clicking and dragging to the new square.  You can also delete the meal by pressing the delete key or right clicking and selecting delete.

How do I make new meals?

Click on new meal at the top of the page.  You will be taken to a new window which will show all the information needed to make a new meal.  For your convience the specific instructions for making a meal are shown on the right side of the window. 

How do I edit existing meals?

Select the desired meal and then right click.  A menu will pop up.  Select Edit Meal and a the meal editing  window will appear.  You can change any attribute that you wish.  Click File/Save to finish.

How do I run a plan?

 Select your desired plan and then right click.  Select Run Plan and the plan will load the entries for the week starting with today.
