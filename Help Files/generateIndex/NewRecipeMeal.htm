How do I enter a new food?

Click the New Food button to bring up the dialog.  If your new food is a recipe then go to this tutorial.   Enter the name type in the name of the food that you wish to enter.  A drop down box will show you all the foods that have already been entered.  If your food is not already in the database, you will be given a blank page.

Food Group:   Now choose the food group that you wish to assign this food item.  If you are not sure, just use what is the majority ingredient in the food.

Serving Information: Enter here the serving size as is listed in your ingredient information.  The number of grams in a serving should be listed here.  If you do not know how many grams are in the serving, just enter 100.

MacroNutrients: Now just copy down all the information from the label as it is listed.  If you do not know an item, just skip it.  Only the calories are required to be entered.

Vitamins: Last, you need to enter the percent information for all of the vitamins.  Just enter those items that you can.  Most nutrition information labels only list a few.

 

And click save to put the food in the database.

 

How do I enter a new recipe?

Click the new recipe, ,  button to the left to enter the recipe.

 


First click the recipe button.  This will bring up a new dialog. 

Now you can enter in the recipe name.  Similar names will be shown, if your food is not already in the database, finish entering the name and then press enter.

Entering ingredients

Enter all the ingredients like you would normally. 

Entering Instructions

Click the Instructions tab and then enter in all the nessasary information.  If you do not know how many grams are in a serving, leave that number like it is.  You can type in the instructions if you need to have them for later, or you plan on uploading them to the website.

If you wish to see the results, you can go to Quick View.

Finally, save the recipe to the database.

 

How do I enter a new meal?

Click the new meal button to the left to get to the dialog.

First enter in a descriptive meal name.  

Enter in the most likely time this meal is going to be eaten.

Type out instructions, for example, when to prepare the ingrediants, suggestions about what drinks to include, or anything that may be helpful.

click on the Ingredients tab and then enter in the food stuff.  

There are suggestions for a complete meal.

Click on Save to finish

 

You may need to enter a new recipe as you are entering a meal.  Or you may find that you need to enter a new ingrediant into the database.  Just click New Food at the top to enter a new food and then return to the meal planner.

How do I upload a recipe?

    To upload a recipe to the to the website, click the web button.  At the bottom of  the webpage you can select the recipe that you wish to upload.  You will be taken to the upload dialog.  Fill in the information at the top of the page for the recipes categories.  Make sure that the instructions are clear and then then click submit.  The recipe will be loaded to the server.

How do I download a recipe?

   To download a recipe, click the web button.  Then on the webpage, click the Recipes link. Find the recipe that you would like to download. At the top of the recipe is a link "Download Recipe to Program".  If you cannot find this link, then the recipe has not been translated to a form that the program understands yet.  We are working on getting all the recipes translated at this time.  After you have clicked the link, the recipe will be downloaded.

How do I delete a food?

   To delete a food, click the new food link at the left.  Type in the name of the food that you wish to delete.  The foods properties will be shown.  Last click Delete at the top of the form and you will be asked if you are sure.  Confirm your choice and the food will be deleted.

How do I delete a meal?

   To delete a meal, select the meal in the meal toolbar to the right of the calorie counter or the meal planner.  After you have selected the meal, right click the meal and select Delete Meal.  The meal will be deleted.

How do I delete a recipe?

   Click the recipes button to the left.  Enter the name of the recipe you wish to delete.  The recipes ingredients will be shown.  Click the delete menu item at the top if the page.  The recipe will be deleted.
