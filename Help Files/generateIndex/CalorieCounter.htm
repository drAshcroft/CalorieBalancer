      The calorie tracker makes it easy to control your weight. We have over 5000 foods from every category from fast food to vegetables. Everything at your fingertips, in a quick and easy format. The database also learns your preferences to make it easier to use all the time.

The Calorie Tracker

How do I enter my foods?


      Click on the apple to the left to navigate to the calorie tracker. Now click on an open space under Foodname. You now can enter your food. A dropdown box will show all the foods that are available in the database. When you see your desired food, either arrow down or click it with the mouse to select. The program will prompt you for your desired Unit and then the Serving Size.

I want to enter foods for a different day.

      Locate the calendar at the lower right.  You can select any day from this calendar.  Just clicking a new day will save the current day's food and then open the next one.

I want to review the last time I entered food.

     If you want to go back the last entry, click File and then Review Last Entry.  The calorie counter will move back to the last entry.

How do I enter my meal?

     After a meal has been created as shown here, It can be placed in the calorie counter.  This is done by using the toolbar on the right.  After selecting the desired meal, you can either drag the meal to the calorie counter, or double click the meal.  The meal will then be automatically loaded according the options set in the Options menu at the top of the page.

Why does the program change the order of the meals?

      The program is designed to help organize things.  If you wish to enter the meals in a different order, click Options at the top and then make sure that Order Meals is unchecked.  The program will then allow you enter the meals in any desired order.  A related option is to Replace Meals.  When the meals are dropped into the calorie counter, the meal is replaced.  When this option is unchecked then the program will enter multiples of the same meal.

I want to enter two snacks/treats.

      If you have the Order and Replace options on, which are described above, but still want to enter multiples of the same meal, you can override the default behavior by pressing shift while dragging the meal into the calorie counter.

What is the difference between and normal meal and a family meal?

      A family meal can be used by all users of the program.  A normal meal is only visible to you.  You can move a meal from one plan to another by dragging the meal to the new location.  You must drop it in the correct meal slot.

How do I create family meals?

       After creating a meal as described above, drag the meal from your personal account to the family meal.

How do I delete a row?

       There are two ways to delete rows.  First right click on the desired meal and select Delete Meal.  The second manner is to highlight the foodnames that you wish to delete and then press the delete key.

How do I enter a food that is not in the database?

Click the new food button to the left. A new window will pop up.

Enter the Foodname at the top of the page. A drop box will be shown that will give similar options.  You can ignore this for now. Now select a Foodgroup and enter in as much information as possible. The entry form is designed to look like a food label on the side of the package. Work your way down the page entering all the information that you know.  You do not need to enter all the information.  Grams should be listed on the side food label, but if you cannot find the number just enter 100. Click Save to finish.

How can I enter a recipe?

Click the new Recipe button to the left. A new window will pop up.
  
Enter the name of the recipe in Foodname. Next click the checkbox to the right labeled, “This is a Recipe”.
The form will change to reveal the recipe editor. Enter the ingredients as you would enter your food items.

Next click on the Instructions tab. This will allow you to enter in the number of servings and serving size for your recipe. If you do not know the grams per serving, enter 100. Click Save to finish.

If you wish to upload the recipe to the website, click the Upload Recipe Tab and follow the instructions.

How do I edit a Food?

    Click the new food button to the left. A new window will pop up. Enter the Foodname of the food you wish to edit at the top of the page. Select the desired option from the dropdown box. You can change anything that is incorrect and then click Save to finish.  If you wish to rename, delete or copy the food then the options are at the top of the page.

Change from calories to percents?

      You may wish to see the percent of the days calories consumed by each food instead of the calories.  Click Options and then Show Nutrients as Percent.  Use the same method to switch back to the default calories

Change the nutrients that are shown with each food?

      


    You make wish to track different nutrients to help you meet your personal goals.  You can adjust the nutrients that are tracked by clicking File, and then Edit Current User.  Scroll down the page and find the section that shows all the nutrients.  Click the nutrients that you wish to track and then click save at the bottom of the page.
