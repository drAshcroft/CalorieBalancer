The Internet Search Page

   There are times that you cannot find the information you need in our database.  You can often find this information on the internet.  This is a one time, instant help page.  This will not appear next time you open this page.

This dialog makes entering information easy.  First, you will be shown the dialog with the Yahoo search engine.  Enter the food for which you are searching.  Once you have found the nutrition information on the internet,

 Now click the transfer button to move the information into the program.  The program will do its best to read the information from the web.  Remember, this is artificial intelligence, so it will miss some of the information, usually the serving information and the foodname.  You will need to look at the information to the right to make sure that everything is entered.  Once all the information is set, Click the Save button at the bottom right or the Save menu item at the top.
